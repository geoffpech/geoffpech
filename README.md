# 💫 About Me:
My name is Geoffrey, I was born on October 24, 1993. After working for ten years in public works 🏗️, I decided to take a new path and pursue something I'm truly passionate about 🎯. As a long-time video game enthusiast 🎮, I became curious about how the tech world behind it all works 💻. This curiosity sparked a passion for web development 🌐. Driven by this newfound ambition, I embarked on a career change and completed a web developer training program 👨‍💻. Though a novice, I am highly motivated and passionate 🚀, and I’m excited to share my GitHub, which reflects this new chapter of my life 📂.
